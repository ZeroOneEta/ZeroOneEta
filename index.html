Engineering from first principles.

I hold a PhD in signal processing from the IT University of Copenhagen. My work centres on four things: digital signal processing, acoustics, beamforming, and deep learning — from microphone-array geometry and room-impulse modelling to neural networks trained on raw signals.

With experience spanning Europe, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific, I've consulted for research labs, startups, and enterprises. Every engagement begins at the mathematics and ends with working, deployed code — not just a notebook.

Currently available remotely for contract work globally — from focused technical spikes to multi-month engagements. CET timezone, 24h response time.

// who I work with

    
Built for research-driven teams.

    
My work sits where peer-reviewed acoustics meets shipping product. These are the kinds of R&D teams and deep-tech startups I'm a strong fit for — each domain backed by my published, cited research.


    


      

        

        
Hearing Aids & Hearables

        
Multi-microphone beamforming, acoustic echo estimation, and DOA estimation for hearing devices and smart earbuds — extracting a target voice from noise.

        
For: hearing-device & hearable R&D teams (Demant · GN · WS Audiology · Sonova‑class)

        
Backed by: EM methods for multichannel TOA/DOA estimation of acoustic echoes

      


      

        

        
Voice AI & Far-Field Capture

        
Far-field speech enhancement, ego-noise suppression, and deep-learning denoising — the front-end signal chain that makes voice interfaces robust in the real world.

        
For: voice-AI, far-field ASR & noise-cancellation startups (Krisp‑class)

        
Backed by: Sound-based distance estimation in the presence of ego noise

      


      

        

        
Robot & Drone Audition

        
Acoustic mapping and SLAM from echoes, reflector localisation, and audio-based perception for platforms that need to sense space through sound.

        
For: autonomous-robot & drone makers, acoustic-SLAM deep-tech startups

        
Backed by: Spatial map generation using acoustic echoes for robotic platforms (23 citations)

      


      

        

        
Spatial & Immersive Audio

        
Room impulse-response modelling, reverberation, and acoustic reflector estimation for realistic spatial audio in AR/VR and immersive experiences.

        
For: spatial-audio teams at AR/VR, gaming & immersive-media labs

        
Backed by: Estimation of acoustic echoes using expectation-maximization methods

      


      

        

        
Indoor Navigation & Sensing

        
Model-based localisation, distance estimation, and array signal processing for GPS-denied indoor navigation and acoustic sensing systems.

        
For: indoor-positioning & sensing startups; array-processing R&D

        
Backed by: Model-based acoustic reflector localization with a robotic platform

      


      

        

        
Consumer & Pro Audio

        
Microphone-array design, adaptive filtering, and DSP/deep-learning pipelines for speakers, conferencing hardware, and professional audio products.

        
For: speaker, microphone & conferencing-hardware R&D (B&O · Sonos · Shure‑class)
